Ginza Kaigari, May 5, 2020

Pictured above: Ginza Kagari in Tokyo, Japan.

I had the pleasure of visiting Japan last June and it was absolutely amazing. One of the most memorable aspects of the trip was the food, specifically the ramen which we ate a lot of. Of the five ramen shops we visited, Ginza Kagari was one of the best. The broth is rich and creamy and the noodles are cooked to perfection. While it is pricey, it is definitely worth the trip.
